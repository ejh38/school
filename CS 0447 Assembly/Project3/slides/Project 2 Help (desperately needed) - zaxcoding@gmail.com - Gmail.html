They look pretty cool. Too bad they're not what you expect. I haven't seen a bug like this before, so I can't tell you why this is happening.

I did code your written solution though and, except for 3 small changes, I got the expected sphere. One change was drawing a quad with vertices in order verts[i][j], [i+1][j], [i+1][j+1], [i][j+1], another was starting theta at 0 rather -PI/2 and, finally, storing the points in verts[j][i] order rather than verts[i][j] in make_sphere.

Even without these changes, maybe you may get half a sphere, but nothing like what you're getting. If you send me your code, I can take a look at it and possibly give you some hints as to what's wrong with it.


Adrian
